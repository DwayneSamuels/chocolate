Plain Dark Chocolate

Our 70% dark chocolate bar is handcrafted using single-origin Jamaican beans from the blue mountains. The 70% dark bar is naturally smooth and has a mellowingly balanced taste. It has particularly fruity notes of tropical blue mountain mist calmness that is typical of Jamaican pineapple and fruits. Our formula and balance of vanilla bring out the uniqueness of our 70% bar taste and the mouthful taste experience.

Ingredients: Cocoa, cocoa butter, cane sugar, and natural vanilla.
